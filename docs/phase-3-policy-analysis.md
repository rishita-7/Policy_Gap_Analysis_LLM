## Objective
Analyze organizational policy documents and identify gaps
by comparing them against structured policy clauses
aligned with the NIST Cybersecurity Framework.

## Inputs
- Organizational policy document (TXT or DOCX)
- Policy clauses benchmark dataset (JSON)

## Key Steps
1. Load and preprocess policy text
2. Segment policy into sentences or sections
3. Extract key terms using NLP techniques
4. Compare policy content against benchmark clauses
5. Classify each clause as Covered, Partially Covered, or Missing

## Output
- Structured gap analysis report
- Mapping of identified gaps to NIST CSF functions
